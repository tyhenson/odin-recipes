Fried Chicken

Simple fried chicken recipe that will have your family thinking you bought take-out

This is my all-time favorite fried chicken recipe. We first started making this recipe back when we were young adults just starting out. We built upon it over the years as our cooking skills developed. It is now a family favorite that is requested more often than people should be eating fried foods.

The ingredients are as simple as the chicken is delicious. Just a bit of flour, some buttermilk, and a few select spices is all you need to make this amazing dish for your family tonight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 egg (room temp)
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fine ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp poultry seasoning
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces (roughly 4 lb)
  • 3 cups of peanut oil (canola or vegetable oil can be substituted in case of allergies)
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of milk
